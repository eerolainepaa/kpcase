Guestions for the stakeholders


### Hotel CEO

1. What are the biggest concerns regarding this investment?
    - price, guest satisfaction, easy to install / maintain
2. How does this lighting system fit to hotel image?
    - Is the hotel high-end or just for average-joes?
3. What is the lifetime of lighting system and for wristband?
    - Do i need to buy new lights and wristbands every month?
4. What is the concerns for data privacy and cybersecurity?
    - Is it possible to use this system to get access in to hotels files and network

### Hotel Guest

1. Does the wristband improve the guest's stay in hotel?
    - Is it too complex to use and is it worth it
2. What happens if the guest loses the wristband?
    - Can it be turned off remotely
3. Is the wristband comfortable to use and wear?
    - What is the material for wristband and style
4. How will the guest be educated for use of wristband?
    - Does it need a manual for use, or can the reseption quide enough

### Architect & interior designer

1. Where is the optimal placement for sensors and lighting?
   - What is the range for sensors and can i use materials that i want
2. Does the lighting system come in differend shapes and colors?
    - Can i put differend lights in hallway than in rooms
3. How does the wristband dock fit in the room
    - Is there only one type of dock
4. What is the technical documentation of lighting system
    - Is it suitable for use

### Maintenance & Electricians & Cleaner

1. How easy is it to replace broken sensor or light
    - Do i need specific tools or is it just plug-and-play
2. What kind of training does the staff need for troubleshooting BLE network
    - Can the problem be located in system or do i need differend tool for it
3. How does the system work in power outage or when the IoT-network is down.
    - Can it be overrided so lighting and doors can be used manually?
4. Is there central dashboard that shows the whole system. Wristband battery status, broken lights etc.
    - Can i just look at one page and see the whole status

### Manufacturing Partner

1. Can the manufacturer scale production if hotel chain decides to roll out this system globally
    - Is there enough resources in manufacturing
2. What is the lead time for producing all parts
    - How long will it take to make one sensor, wristband and light
3. How do we ensure that hardware meets international certifications
    - What materials are to be used
4. What is the lifespan of the battery in wristband and can it be changed
