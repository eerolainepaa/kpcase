## Prototype description
Prototype is web-based UI, that simulates the lighting control. The prototype contains an example of a floor plan, which shows the placements of the lighting and a cursor works as a guest wristband and show the actions of going in and leaving the room. There are also buttons implemented for examples of different lighting themes, which are shown visually on the floor plan. The web-based UI is a first example for the contol pannel of the maintenance staff and will also include warning for faulty lights and where they are located.
The prototype is not connected to real lights or database. The UI reacts for input visually directly from the screen.
